About the Club

TWelcome to our dance club! We are thrilled to have you join us in the art of dance. Our club is dedicated to providing a fun, supportive, and inclusive environment for dancers of all levels and backgrounds to come together and express themselves through movement. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or just starting out, we have something for everyone. Our experienced instructors are passionate about teaching and will guide you through a variety of dance styles, from salsa to hip-hop to ballroom. Join us and let's dance our way to a healthier, happier, and more connected community!

Classes

The Dance Club offers classes every Tuesday and Thursday from 16h to 18h. Each class costs 20 euros. We offer classes in K-pop, hip hop, and ballet.

K-pop Class

Learn the latest K-pop dance routines from our experienced instructors.

Hip Hop Class

Bust a move with our high-energy hip hop classes.

Ballet Class

Master the grace and precision of ballet with our classical dance classes.

Membership

To become a member of the Dance Club, simply attend one of our classes and pay the class fee. You can also purchase a membership for a discounted rate.

  • Single Class Fee: 20 euros
  • Monthly Membership: 100 euros (includes unlimited classes)
